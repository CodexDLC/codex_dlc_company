## 1. Fluid Branding (DLC Matrix)
The "D.L.C." acronym is dynamic and adapts to the context of the page.

* **Digital Lead Conversion:** Home Page / CTA. Focus: Sales and results.
* **Digital Local Clients:** WaaS / Subscription page. Focus: Local small businesses.
* **Digital Life Creation:** About Us / Footer. Focus: Global mission.
* **Developer's Life Cycle:** Tech Corner / Console. Focus: Engineering expertise.

## 2. Localization Strategy (DACH Focus)
* **DE (Primary):** Business value, GDPR (Datenschutz), uptime, and transparent pricing for local businesses.
* **EN (Secondary):** Architectural documentation, system specs, CI/CD, and international image.

## 3. Technical SEO
* **Schema.org:** JSON-LD for `SoftwareApplication` and `ProfessionalService`.
* **Performance:** 100/100 Core Web Vitals using HTMX and SSR.
* **Semantic HTML:** Strict header hierarchy (H1-H3) with technical keywords.

## 4. Media & Assets
* **SVG Only:** All diagrams and icons. No raster graphics for technical illustrations.
* **WebP:** Compressed screenshots of prototypes (e.g., LILY Salon).
* **CSS Animations:** Lightweight transitions. No heavy animation libraries. Focus on results, not "process".
